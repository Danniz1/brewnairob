Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know before your first (or fiftieth) visit

General

No reservation needed for regular visits — we operate on a first-come, first-served basis. For groups of 8 or more, or for our private meeting room, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance via the Contact page.

We're open Monday to Friday from 7:00am to 9:00pm, Saturday from 8:00am to 9:00pm, and Sunday from 9:00am to 6:00pm. Hours may vary slightly on public holidays — check our Instagram for any changes.

Yes — there's free street parking along Kilimani Road, and a small paid parking lot directly behind our building that charges KSh 100 per hour.

Yes, free fibre Wi-Fi throughout the space, and most tables have nearby power outlets. The back section is specifically arranged for remote workers and laptops.

Menu & Dietary

Yes — we offer oat milk, almond milk, and soy milk for an additional KSh 80. Oat milk is our most popular alternative and steams especially well for latte art.

We source directly from eleven smallholder farms across Nyeri, Kirinyaga, and Murang'a in Kenya's Central Highlands. Every bean is roasted in-house, usually within two weeks of arrival. Read more on our farm visit blog post.

Our avocado toast and banana bread are vegetarian, and can be made vegan on request (vegan butter substitute, no honey drizzle). Ask your barista — most of our pastries can be adapted with a little notice.

Yes — all our single origins are available as 250g retail bags at the counter, whole bean or ground to your preferred brew method. Prices range from KSh 950 to KSh 1,400 depending on the origin.

Events & Community

Every Saturday at 10am, our head roaster leads a free, open-to-the-public cupping of two or three current single origins. No booking required — just show up a few minutes early, space is limited to about 15 people.

Yes — we run beginner espresso and latte art workshops on the last Sunday of every month. Sessions are KSh 2,500 per person and include all materials. Message us via the Contact page to reserve a spot.

Our private meeting room seats up to 10 and can be booked for KSh 3,000 for a half-day, which includes unlimited coffee for your group. For larger events or full-venue bookings, reach out through the Contact page with your date and group size.

Still have a question?

We're happy to help — send us a message and we'll get back to you within a day.
